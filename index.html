About Us

Welcome to Pizza Ricotta, Calicut’s favourite destination for mouth-watering, handcrafted pizzas! Located in the heart of the city, we pride ourselves on delivering not just food, but an experience. Our pizzas are crafted with the freshest ingredients, topped with our signature ricotta cheese, and baked to perfection. At Pizza Ricotta, we believe in quality, taste, and tradition. Whether you're craving a classic Margherita or something more adventurous, our diverse menu has something for every palate. We also offer a range of sides, desserts, and beverages to complement your meal. We know how important it is to enjoy a delicious pizza in the comfort of your own home, which is why we offer fast and reliable delivery services across Calicut. With just a few clicks, you can have your favourite pizza delivered right to your doorstep. Join us for a slice of happiness at Pizza Ricotta – where every bite is a celebration of flavour!
